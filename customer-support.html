ZapManejo guides you through each phase of the IATF protocol (D0 through D11) via WhatsApp messages. Simply respond to the prompts in your ZapManejo conversation to log implants, hormones, insemination dates, and pregnancy confirmation. Voice messages are also supported. Contact support if you need a walkthrough of your first protocol.
Yes, ZapManejo supports RFID integration via Bluetooth HID readers. You can scan RFID tags directly into the WhatsApp interface, linking each tag to an animal's complete record. For setup assistance, contact our support team who can walk you through the pairing process for your specific reader model.

Yes. You can request an export of all your cattle data at any time during an active subscription. Records are exported in CSV format compatible with spreadsheet applications. Contact support to initiate an export.
