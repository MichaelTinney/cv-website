Privacy Policy

Catalytic Verticals LLC & ZapManejo

Last Updated: June 1, 2025  |  Effective Date: June 1, 2025

Catalytic Verticals LLC ("Catalytic Verticals," "we," "our," or "us"), a Florida limited liability company, operates ZapManejo and related products and services (collectively, the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information about you when you use our Services.

By accessing or using our Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our Services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Registration: Name, email address, phone number (including WhatsApp number), company or farm name, and location.
  • Livestock & Farm Data: Animal identification numbers, ear tag IDs, RFID data, weight records, health records, breeding records, IATF protocol data, and other agricultural management data you enter into ZapManejo.
  • Communications: Messages you send via WhatsApp to our platform, support requests, and feedback.
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed through our payment partners (Stripe, EBANX/Pix). We do not store raw payment card numbers.
  • Contact Form Submissions: Name, company, email, industry, and project scope details submitted via our website.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Log files, IP addresses, device type, operating system, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps.
  • WhatsApp Interaction Data: Message metadata, response times, and feature usage patterns (not message content beyond what you send to our platform).
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies on our website for analytics and functionality. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

1.3 Information From Third Parties

  • WhatsApp Business API data provided by Meta Platforms, Inc.
  • Payment transaction data from Stripe and EBANX.
  • Analytics data from web analytics providers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our Services, including ZapManejo livestock management features.
  • Process transactions and send related notices (receipts, invoices, payment confirmations).
  • Send operational communications such as service updates, security alerts, and support responses.
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Monitor and analyze usage to improve platform performance and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or abuse.
  • Send marketing communications where you have given consent (you may opt out at any time).

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist in operating our Services (cloud hosting, payment processing, analytics, customer support tools), subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • WhatsApp / Meta: As required to deliver WhatsApp Business API functionality. Use is subject to Meta's Business Terms of Service.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect the rights and safety of Catalytic Verticals, our users, or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with appropriate confidentiality protections.
  • With Your Consent: In any other circumstance where you have given explicit consent.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide Services. Livestock and farm management data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus a 90-day grace period after termination. We may retain certain data longer as required by law or for legitimate business purposes such as dispute resolution. You may request deletion of your data as described in Section 6.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (TLS), secure data storage, and access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and notify us immediately of any suspected unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable, machine-readable format.
  • Objection / Restriction: Object to or request restriction of certain processing activities.
  • Marketing Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time via the unsubscribe link in emails or by contacting us.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@catalyticverticals.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Brazilian Users – LGPD Compliance

For users in Brazil, we comply with the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD – Law No. 13,709/2018). The legal bases for processing your data include performance of a contract, legitimate interests, compliance with legal obligations, and, where required, your explicit consent. You have the rights enumerated in Article 18 of the LGPD, which you may exercise by contacting us at the address below.

8. WhatsApp Data

Our Services are delivered via the WhatsApp Business API. By using ZapManejo through WhatsApp, you also agree to Meta's Privacy Policy and WhatsApp's Terms of Service. We process WhatsApp messages only for the purposes of delivering the Services you have requested. We do not use WhatsApp message content for advertising.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Catalytic Verticals is based in the United States. If you access our Services from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure such transfers comply with applicable law.

11. Third-Party Links

Our website and communications may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and, where appropriate, by email or WhatsApp notification. Your continued use of our Services after the effective date of any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
